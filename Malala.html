The Book

Malala, I have grown up hearing her name and I know she got shot by a bullet but this is a strange world we live in and anything can happen here. So, I didn't think about it alot

Reading her story got me thinking about how brave humans can be. A girl who got to speak at the United Nations and got a chance to change the world

But maybe we all have that chance. We are choosing not to be there
