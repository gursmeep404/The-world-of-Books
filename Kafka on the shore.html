Kafka on the shore

The Book

This is the strangest book I have read this summer. So many loose endings and mostly for the reader to interpret

So many lives and so many perspectives. To be honest it was a little disturbing at times but a beautiful story it was

Kafka Tamura's tale of finding himself. The parts I loved the most were the painting of kafka and the song kafka on the shore

  • "What am I always so tensed about? Why this desperate struggle just to survive”
  • "Silence I discover is something you can actually hear "
  • “She gazes- At Kafka on the shore”
