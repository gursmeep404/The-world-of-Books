The Things We Leave Unfinished

The Book

Four generations, three romances, two endings and one story. But the story that I loved the most was about Scarlett and Jameson.

Two people who met each other in the midst of World War II and lived half their life in letters. She was a section officer and he was a fighter pilot of the US army.

It all starts in UK but ends in colorado but if you truly ask me, for me the story is stuck on the station where Scarlett and Jamesson met, on their first sunset date, their first dance and their letters to each other
